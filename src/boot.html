Everything Grandkids

Do!

Spirited, empathetic youth companions for the elderly.

We are a group of young graduates who have an affection towards the elderly and look forward to creating meaningful bonds with our Grandpals and as we say, we would do everything that your grandkid would do for you!

Discover the joy of Quality time

Be it nothing but chitchat, reminiscing stories, sharing troubles, or biscuits and tea, playing carrom together or simply watching TV, our Goodfellows are eager to lend a listening ear.

Bridge generation gap with tech-savvy youth

Your Goodfellow will sit down for as long as you need to explain the basics of smartphones and gadgets, social media lingo and online deliveries. You name it, we teach it!

Find Joy in Walking together

We surely love a good walk would love to keep you company. Be it escorting you to a friend’s house or being your friend at the park, we have your back.

Companionship for Daily tasks

Getting your daily supplies, medicines or shopping at the mall? Our Goodfellows make excellent shopping companions. Or let your Goodfellow accompany you to the doctor’s clinic. It is always good to have someone in the waiting room.

Discover the Thrill of something new

Try an experience at that fancy restaurant or watch a new movie, learn a new hobby, or explore an old one! Our Goodfellows will take you to the best of places for an outing of your choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?


We’re so glad you’re here! To get started, click on the sign-up button that says ‘Join The Trial’ and fill in the form that appears. Our team will reach out to you within 48 hours to match you with your perfect companion. If you need any assistance, please reach out to us at +91 8779524307 from Monday to Friday between 10 AM to 6 PM. We’re always here to help.


Is this a paid service? Why?


Your first few sessions with the Goodfellow is entirely free. If you are happy, you can continue with a paid subscription. If you do not wish to continue, there will be no money charged. Your Goodfellows have been vetted for empathy and have an affinity towards elderlies, but like any other graduate, they would like to feel secure about their finances and do their best for you. To give them dignified, respectable salaries for their time invested, this is a paid service. This also ensures their longstanding association with us in order to fulfill an enduring companionship with you.
