We are the Goodfellows

Welcome to our Careers page, We're looking for individuals who are driven, passionate, and empathetic. People who understand that being a companion is more than just keeping someone company - it's about truly caring for another person and making a positive impact in their life. As a member of our team, you'll receive ongoing training and support to help you grow both professionally and personally.

We'll work with you to identify your strengths and help you develop the skills you need to excel in your role. You'll also have the opportunity to make a real difference in the lives of our Grandpals, and experience the satisfaction that comes with knowing you've made a positive impact.

Together, we can bring joy and companionship to the lives of senior citizens.
